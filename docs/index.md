# Siklab Core

**Describe what you need. Get a working app.**

Siklab Core takes a request in plain language — _"build me a simple invoice tracker for my freelance work"_ — and delivers a finished, ready-to-use app. You don't write code, wrangle servers, or hire a team. You describe what you want, refine an interactive preview, and receive the finished app with a guide for using it.

---

## How it works

```
Describe it ──▶ Review the plan ──▶ Refine a live preview ──▶ Get your app
```

1. **Describe it.** Pick what you're doing — build an app, add a feature, fix a problem, or plan a project — and say it in your own words. Siklab helps you sharpen the request as you type.
2. **Review the plan.** Your idea is restated back to you in plain language, with a clear approach. You approve it before anything happens.
3. **Refine a live preview.** You get an **interactive preview** you can actually click through, and you shape it in conversation — _"count glasses, not milliliters"_ — until it's right.
4. **Get your app.** Once you approve, Siklab builds the real thing and hands it back, along with a plain-language guide. Everything you've made is collected in one place.

---

## Your team behind the scenes

Siklab isn't one chatbot — it's a coordinated team, each with a job:

- **Mayari** frames your idea and keeps it on track.
- **Tala** designs how it looks and feels.
- **Apolaki** builds it.
- **Sidapa** checks that it actually works before it reaches you.

You mostly talk to one voice; the rest happens for you.

---

## What makes it different

- **No code, no setup headaches.** Install the app, set a password, connect your AI engine — that's the whole setup. No database to run, no server to manage.
- **You steer it.** Interactive previews let you shape the result *before* it's built for real, so you get what you actually meant.
- **You own the result.** Delivered apps are yours to open and keep.
- **It stays with you.** Add features, fix issues, or plan the next thing — in the same place, in plain language.

---

## Built to a standard

Most AI tools generate code and hope. Siklab is powered by **LASDK** — the engine that governs every build: planning it, checking it, and verifying it works before it ever reaches you. You get software that's been held to a standard, not a first draft you have to debug.
